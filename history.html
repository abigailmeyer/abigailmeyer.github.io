The History behind Alex Madonna and the Madonna Inn

Real estate developers create developments from the ground up and these developments are architecture. The opening of the Madonna Inn took place in 1958 around Christmas time, at this time there were only 12 rooms built according to the Inns website, the people who had booked for the grand opening were given a complimentary nights stay. Since then the Madonna Inn now has one hundred and ten rooms total.

Alex Madonna was not only the thoughts behind this amazing idea but also the architect. Alex Madonna did not hire an architect for this project of his.
