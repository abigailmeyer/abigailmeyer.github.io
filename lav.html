LAVENDER

Lavender flowers are known to represent purity, silence, devotion serenity, grace, and calmness. This photo was taken in Normandy, France, summer of 2018. My father was born in Verdun, France a place filled with history, after my father retired we spent the summer backpacking around France and came across many lavender fields. I chose to use this photo because this time in my life was very eyeopening and graceful, similar to the representation of the lavender.
