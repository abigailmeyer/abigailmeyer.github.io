From avocado toast to tortilla chips a toaster is your best friend! A toaster can be used in so many different ways if known. We want you guys to be able to take advantage of all the things a toaster can do and we want you to share with us how you use a toaster.

A TOASTER ACCESORY YOU NEED


Let me tell you about toaster bags, if you have a toaster and don’t own any toaster bags these will be a life-changer for you and your toaster. There are so many benefits to using toaster bags, they keep your toaster super clean and you can utilize your toaster in so many new ways when you have toaster bags, which allows you to cook a lot of things in your kitchenless dorm room. Amazon is a great place to buy toaster bags and they’re super cheap. With toaster bags, you can make tortillas chips, grilled cheese sandwiches, unthaw frozen meat like sausage patties, and veggie burgers, and you can even make french fries! After reading this I’m hoping you’ll become apart of toaster bag culture.
